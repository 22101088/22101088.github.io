The duties of a Cell Phone Repair Technician are to troubleshoot issues with mobile phones, diagnose the cause of a particular problem, then replace parts or perform software updates to repair the device and restore it to full functionality.
The average cost to fix a phone in the UK, whether Android or iOS, can range from £15 to £265. This is dependent on the repair needed to fix the phone, the certified required parts and the labour costs.
What skills do you need to be a mobile technician?
Excellent hand-eye coordination and ability to perform small repairs.
In-depth knowledge of different models of cell phones both old and new.
Capable of following instructions reading diagrams and carrying out tasks.
Professional demeanor with customers and coworkers.
